Black & Decker PAV1200W 12-Volt Cyclonic-Action Automotive Pivoting-Nose Handheld Vacuum Cleaner


PAV1200W Automotive Pivot Vac
View larger
Easy Clean up for your Car, Truck or Boat

The Black & Decker PAV1200W Automotive Pivot Vac makes cleaning your vehicle or boat much easier, as this unit plugs into 12V vehicle outlet for plenty of power to pick up gravel, dirt, crumbs and more. It features a slim pivoting nozzle that lets you hand vacuum from any angle and access hard-to-reach spaces that those snacks, treats, and dirt find their way into. It also offers an extended 16' power cord that allows you to reach anywhere you need to clean inside your vehicle.


Specifications:
  • Voltage 12V
  • Air Watts 11 watts
  • Capacity 11.8 oz.
  • Cord Length 16 ft.
  • Replacement Filter PVF100
Convenient Cord Storage
View larger
Power and Convenience

This vac features cyclonic action which means the filter stays clean and the suction stays stronger for a longer amount of time. The one-step easy-empty bagless dirt canister allows for for a mess-free clean up, meaning your hands don't have to touch the dirt and mess! It has a convenient filter refresh knob that allows you to clear debris off filter to improve suction power, and the 3-Stage filtration prevents dust from escaping into the air. The hand vac conveniently folds in half with a cord wrap tool for easy and compact storage that keeps the cord neat and tidy.

What's included: Automotive handheld vacuum with 16 ft cord, Crevice tool, Upholstery brush, Flexible hose, Dishwasher safe filter (PVF100), Plastic pre filter, and Storage bag.

The BEST auto vacuum
By Howie on April 19, 2009
Verified Purchase
This thing is perfect! The things I looked for when shopping for my first auto vac were:

1) Length of cord - plenty of legnth. DON'T EVER GO FOR A WIRELESS AUTO VAC! The suction power dies, and eventually, you'll have 5 minutes of good suction per charge. The cord for this vac is too long - and that's a good thing. Of course you'll need a cigarette lighter input, which all cars should have. I am able to reach the back of my Scion tC with ease and extra cord available. And if you know anything about tC's, they are small cars so you big car owners should have no problem at all.

2) Suction power - Since this is a wired-type, there's no need to worry about loss of suction power. With 12V, this thing will clean up your car pretty nicely. For those stubborn pieces of dirt, hair, cloth, etc. that stick to your car fabric, the brush attachment does wonders.

3) Attachments - It comes with three attachments - a brush attachment, a rectangle-to-circle attachment to attach the brush, and a tube attachment to reach those hard-to-reach spots. This vac comes with a pivoting action end which is, in my opinion, genius. It really helps to access under the seats and under the side-ends of the back seats.

4) Convenience - It's lightweight, and comes with a bag, so you can carry it around for travel.

All in all, this is the best auto vac solution. Don't worry about the price - this thing is worth every penny.

Wow! That's the one I was looking for!
By Serg on January 20, 2010
Verified Purchase
It's the third vacuum cleaner I have experience with. First one was cheap and useless even it was plugged in cigarette lighter it never could pick up any dirt, the second was the same but wireless with 3 minutes of full suction and 2 minutes of slow death (30 min to recharge). I'm done with cheap (around $12) stuff. It was all wasting of money and I always ended up with brush cleaning. Even gas station vacuum cleaners with their nasty hoses never could done the job what I done with this fine product: Black & Decker PAV1200.
It was my first expression after I just started cleaning: "WOW!!!" and the same when I was done "Impressive!!!".
I came with two brushes, hose and adapter for hose connection. But it also has a nice feature as moving and extensible nozzle. You can adjust its position for your convenience and after you done you can fold it what makes whole unit is compact. If you look at the picture of cleaner you can see the red tip on the end of nozzle. That one is extensible to about 4-5". And its tinny so you can reach all slits.
I used one brush on this tip extending nozzle and I was done with cleaning of my minivan in 10 minutes reaching all spots including carpet mats, under pedals, under seats, between seats and decor. I got everything: fluff and hair with brush tip, dust and crumbs with cleaner suction which is pretty decent.
Cord is pretty long and easy to reel on back after you done.
Cleaner comes with nice bag with handle, so all parts including the unit you can keep together and it does not take to much room.

I Highly recommend this unit for everyone who got tired of search of decent vacuum cleaners and want to keep his car clean.

P.S. I cleaned with car engine running (did not want to waste my battery)
